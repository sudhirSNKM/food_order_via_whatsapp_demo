Our Story

From a Small Kitchen to 2,500+ Happy Customers

GreenBowl started in 2022 when our founder, Chef Aryan Kapoor, returned from culinary training in Italy with one powerful idea: healthy food should never be boring or inaccessible.

Operating from a cozy cloud kitchen in Koramangala, Bengaluru, we began with just 5 salad recipes and a WhatsApp number. Within 6 months, we were serving over 300 orders daily and had a cult following among health-conscious foodies.

Today, GreenBowl is Bengaluru's most loved salad cloud kitchen ‚Äî offering 25+ signature recipes, daily-sourced local produce, and a commitment to quality that never wavers. Every bowl is a labor of love.
